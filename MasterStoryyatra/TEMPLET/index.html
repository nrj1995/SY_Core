Story Behind My Yatra

Wo gagan ko chumte parvat, wo milo door niharte huye raste, Samudra ke aakhiree
chhor ko dekhate hue sochana… are yaar iske aakge kya hoga or rahasy se bhara hua
ye antariksh. Aap sabhi ki tarah mujhe bhi ye sabhi chize bahot hi fascinating 
lagati hai. When I was in school, I use to day dream of the places to visit and
how people from different geolocation treat with their culture it just wonderful.
To Fir kya.. jab bhi mujhe mauka milta nikal jata kisike sath ya akele kyunki in
raho me tum akele nahi ho dost, raho me aksar kuchh anokhe log mil hi jate hai.
I have experienced that.

Mai jab bhi kabhi apni journey ki shuruat karta mujhe aisa lagta hai ki mai ek
kahani likh raha hoon ya mai uss kahani ko ji(live) raha hnoo. For me every yatra
is a story, to tell the obstacles, challenges, experience and place that I have 
come across. Ussi samay se mai apni har jaourny ko iss StoryYatra par share karta
hoon. I hope you lovely people feel the same way as I am. Please be there with me
because your love motivates me to come up with new yatra.
